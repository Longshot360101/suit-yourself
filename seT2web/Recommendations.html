Recommendations

This section is my recommendation of some the items that have been mentioned and how they should be styled.

Item Opinion Where to find
Suit I would recommend a dark suit as it is timeless and it can be used in many situations. Dark Blue Hugo Boss suit
Shirt I would recommend a light blue shirt as it complements the navy colour of the suit. H&M Light Blue Shirt
Tie I would recommend a striped tie with burgundy, dark blue and light grey as it both complements and contrasts the suit and shirt. Burgundy and Dark Blue Striped Tie
