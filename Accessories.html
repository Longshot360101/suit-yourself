Accessories

Used to add a little flair to your outfit.

Neck Ties

The neck tie or simply known as tie, is a piece of cloth worn around the neck for decorative purposes, resting under the shirt collar and knotted at the throat. There are many different colours and patterns that you can get even the type of knot that is tied. This adds your own personality to what could be a run-of-the-mill suit.

Belts

In terms of wearing a belt it is best to wear either a brown or black leather belt and it should match the colour of your shoes.

Cufflinks

Cufflinks are pieces of jewellery that are used to fasten the cuffs of dress shirts, these must be worn when wearing double cuffs. They come in many styles and sizes, which can be used to show off some of your personality. It is to be noted that in a formal occasion only very simple cufflinks are to be worn, no novelty ones.

Lapel pins

Lapel pins are small, decorative pins that are worn the lapel of a jacket or coat, they are often used to show personal interests, affiliations or add some style to the outfit.
